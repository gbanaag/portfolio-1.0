BRIEF

I redesigned a feature of Uber Eats as part of the application process for the Kleiner Perkins Fellowship.

For the Kleiner Perkins Fellowship Design Challenge, I decided to redesign the ordering process on the Uber Eats app from a UX/UI perspective. Uber eats is a food service app under Uber Technologies that delivers food from local restaurants straight to your door.

RESEARCH

Product research, surveys, and reviews

To identify a problem on the app to explore, I went through three stages of research: conducting product research by reading up on how Uber Eats functions and by testing the app, surveying Uber Eats users, and reading through reviews on the App and Google Play stores.

After undergoing all three stages, the problem I chose to explore was Uber Eats's ordering system.

DEFINING

Ordering presented some underlooked issues.
